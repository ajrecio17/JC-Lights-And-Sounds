About Us

Delivering Excellence in Audio and Lighting for Over 15 Years

Our Story

JC Lights and Sounds was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to bring professional-grade audio and lighting equipment to events across Laguna and beyond. What started as a small equipment rental service has grown into a comprehensive event production company trusted by hundreds of satisfied clients.

Our founder, Jonathan Cruz, recognized a gap in the market for reliable, high-quality sound and lighting solutions. With years of experience in the music and event industries, he built JC Lights and Sounds on principles of excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art facility with cutting-edge equipment, a talented team of technicians, and a proven track record of delivering exceptional results for corporate events, weddings, concerts, conferences, and everything in between.
